Beyond the icon

A committed woman

In the first part of our artistic exploration, we dive into the vibrant political commitment of Frida Kahlo. Through her art, she vigorously defended her political, social and communist convictions. Her paintings have raised heated debates on issues such as social justice, gender equality and economic inequality.

cultural heritage

Frida Kahlo celebrated her Mexican cultural heritage through her art, highlighting the richness and diversity of Mexican culture through her bright colors and indigenous motifs. Her work proudly affirmed his national identity and belonging to a resilient nation, while leaving a lasting impact on modern art.
